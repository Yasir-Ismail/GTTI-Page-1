About the Wing

The Refrigeration and Air Conditioning (RAC) wing is a specialized trade focused on climate control technologies. As global temperatures rise and industrial cooling needs grow, the demand for skilled HVAC technicians is skyrocketing.

Our training covers domestic appliances like refrigerators and split ACs, as well as commercial chillers, cold storage, and central air conditioning systems.
