Institutional Charter

Our commitment to governance, quality, and student success.

Purpose & Mandate

The Government Technical Training Institute (GTTI) Rahim Yar Khan is established with the mandate to provide high-quality technical education and vocational training to the youth of the region. Our charter is rooted in the belief that skilled human capital is the driver of national economic growth.

We are committed to operating under the regulations set forth by TEVTA Punjab, ensuring that all our programs meet national and international standards of competency.

Core Values

Integrity

Upholding transparency and ethical standards in all administrative and academic processes.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in teaching methodology and practical training.

Innovation

Embracing modern technology and industrial trends to keep our curriculum relevant.

Inclusivity

Providing equal opportunities for learning and growth to all segments of society.

Accreditation & Affiliation

GTTI RYK is fully accredited by the National Vocational and Technical Training Commission (NAVTTC) and affiliated with the Punjab Board of Technical Education (PBTE). Our certifications are recognized nationally and provide pathways to international career opportunities.
