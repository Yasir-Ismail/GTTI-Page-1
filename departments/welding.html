Welding Technology

Mastering the Art of Metal Fabrication.

Specialized Wing

About the Wing

The Welding Technology wing focuses on the critical skills needed in the construction, pipeline, and manufacturing sectors. From structural steel to precision pipe welding, we cover it all.

We provide hands-on training in Arc, Gas, TIG, and MIG welding processes, ensuring our graduates are ready for high-demand industrial roles.

Eligibility Criteria

Education

Middle / Matriculation Preferred

Age Limit

18 - 40 Years

Learning Outcomes

Technical Skills

  • SMAW (Shielded Metal Arc Welding).
  • TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) Welding.
  • Gas Cutting and Brazing.

Practical Exposure

  • Overhead and vertical welding positions.
  • Pipe welding (1G to 6G positions).
  • Weld defect identification.

Safety Standards

  • PPE usage (Helmets, Gloves).
  • Fire safety in welding workshops.
