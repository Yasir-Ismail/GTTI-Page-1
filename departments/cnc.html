About the Wing

The CNC (Computer Numerical Control) & Machining wing bridges the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern automated manufacturing. We train students to program and operate high-tech machines that create precision parts for aerospace, automotive, and medical industries.

Learning Outcomes

Technical Skills

  • G-Code and M-Code programming.
  • Setting up CNC Lathe and Milling machines.
  • Understanding geometric tolerances.

Softwares

  • MasterCAM / SolidCAM.

Practical Exposure

  • Machining industrial components.
  • Tool offset calculation.
