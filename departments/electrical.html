Electrical Technology

Powering the Nation with Advanced Electrical Solutions.

Department Profile

About the Department

The Electrical Technology department offers comprehensive training in domestic, commercial, and industrial electrical systems. From basic wiring to complex power distribution networks, we ensure our students master the current.

Our curriculum emphasizes safety, energy efficiency, and modern renewable energy technologies like solar power systems.
