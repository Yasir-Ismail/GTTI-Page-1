Instrumentation Control

Precision Control for Process Industries.

Specialized Wing

About the Wing

The Instrumentation Control wing focuses on the measurement and control of process variables like temperature, pressure, flow, and level. This trade is crucial for chemical plants, oil & gas refineries, and power stations.

Learning Outcomes

Calibration

  • Calibrating pressure gauges and transmitters.
  • Temperature sensor testing (RTD, Thermocouples).

Control Loops

  • Understanding PID controllers.
  • Loop tuning and troubleshooting.

Valves & Actuators

  • Control valve overhaul.
  • Pneumatic actuator maintenance.
