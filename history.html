Our History

Four decades of excellence in technical education and nation building.

1982

Foundation

GTTI Rahim Yar Khan was established with the vision to provide technical skills to the youth of Southern Punjab. Initially offering only 3 trades with a few dozen students.

1995

Expansion

New academic blocks were constructed, and the number of departments increased to 8, including Electrical and Mechanical technologies.

2005

Modernization

Computer labs were upgraded, and IT courses were introduced to keep pace with the digital revolution.

2018

Center of Excellence

Declared a Center of Excellence by the provincial government, recognizing our consistent high performance and placement records.

2024

GTTI SmartHub

Launch of the SmartHub initiative, digitizing campus operations and introducing advanced LMS features for students and faculty.

GTTI SmartHub

The apex body for technical training and vocational excellence.
