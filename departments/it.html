About the Department

The Department of Information Technology at GTTI is dedicated to producing skilled professionals capable of meeting the dynamic demands of the global IT industry. Our curriculum is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and practical software development.

Students gain hands-on experience in modern programming languages, database management, and network security, ensuring they are industry-ready upon graduation.
