About the Wing

Plumbing is a vital trade for modern living. Our department focuses on water supply, drainage, gas fitting, and sanitary systems for both residential and commercial buildings.

Students learn to interpret blueprints, install diverse piping systems, and ensure compliance with health and safety codes.

Learning Outcomes

Technical Skills

  • Pipe threading, cutting, and joining (PPRC, PVC, GI).
  • Installation of sanitary fittings (sinks, commodes).
  • Gas line fitting and safety.

Practical Exposure

  • Water pump installation and repair.
  • Leak detection and drainage solutions.
  • blue-print reading for plumbing layouts.
